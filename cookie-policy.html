What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

How we use them?

  • Essential: Needed for the site to function.
  • Analytics: To understand how you use our site via Google Analytics 4.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to show you relevant ads.

How to control cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may impact site functionality and ad relevance.
